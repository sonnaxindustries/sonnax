For each Power Train Saver series, the following information is provided: driveline series, tube diameter and wall thickness, PTS unit part number, and Torque Fuse® set recommendations.

Every PTS unit requires a Torque Fuse set (typically three fuse links); fuse set recommendations are provided for each PTS unit based on the torque capacity of the driveline series and driveshaft tube in which it will be installed. These recommendations allow buyers to choose fuse shear points based on three specific levels of protection:

  • Standard Protection is the recommended starting point for most applications.
  • Basic Protection uses driveshaft torque capacity as a protection-level target.
  • Maximum Protection aims at protecting weaker components such as differentials or gear boxes.

Your Sonnax sales representative can assist you with fuse set recommendations, particularly if differential protection is your primary concern.

Contact us for more information about bolt-in flanged units for industrial and marine usage, towing disconnect , custom build options and heavy duty bearing-type units for unmanned applications.
